#### **ë‹¨ê³„ 1: Established Knowledge**

> Cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT) is the most extensively studied and empirically supported psychological treatment for major depressive disorder (MDD), with meta-analyses demonstrating medium-to-large effect sizes (d=0.65-0.85) that are comparable to antidepressant medications (31, 32). #### **ë‹¨ê³„ 2: Emerging Challenges**
